Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about QuickMedi services

How do I book an appointment with a doctor?

To book an appointment:

  1. Go to the Book Appointment page
  2. Select your preferred doctor or specialty
  3. Choose an available date and time
  4. Provide your medical information (if required)
  5. Confirm your appointment

You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments:

  • Log in to your account
  • Go to the "Appointments" section
  • Find the appointment you want to change
  • Click "Reschedule" or "Cancel"

Note: Cancellations less than 24 hours before the appointment may be subject to a fee.

How do I order prescription medications?

To order prescription medications:

  1. Visit the Buy Medicine page
  2. Search for your prescribed medication
  3. Upload your prescription (or have your doctor send it directly)
  4. Select quantity and delivery options
  5. Complete your order

Most prescriptions are delivered within 1-3 business days.

What if my medication requires refrigeration?

We handle temperature-sensitive medications with care:

  • Medications are shipped in special insulated packaging
  • Ice packs or dry ice are included as needed
  • Expedited shipping options are available
  • You'll receive tracking information and delivery alerts

Please be available to receive and refrigerate the medication immediately upon delivery.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with many major insurance providers for both medical services and prescriptions:

  • During checkout, you can enter your insurance information
  • We'll verify your coverage and apply benefits
  • You'll only be responsible for any copays or deductibles

For a list of accepted insurance plans, please visit our Insurance Information page.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do I update my personal information?

You can update your information at any time:

  • Log in to your account
  • Go to the "Profile" section
  • Edit any of your personal details
  • Click "Save Changes"

Important: Please keep your contact and insurance information up to date for proper care coordination.

What should I do in a medical emergency?

For life-threatening emergencies:

Call your local emergency number (911 in the US) or go to the nearest hospital immediately.

For urgent but non-life-threatening situations:

  • Use our Emergency Services page to request assistance
  • Visit our Find Hospital page to locate nearby facilities
  • Call our 24/7 support line at +1 (800) 555-1234

How quickly can I get emergency medical help?

Response times vary depending on your location and the nature of the emergency:

  • Ambulance services: Typically 5-15 minutes in urban areas
  • Urgent care: Same-day appointments often available
  • Telemedicine: Immediate connection to a healthcare provider

For the fastest response in life-threatening situations, always call emergency services first.
